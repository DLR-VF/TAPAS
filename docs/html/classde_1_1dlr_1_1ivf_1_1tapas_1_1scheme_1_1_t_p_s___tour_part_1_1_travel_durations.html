Detailed Description

Structure to store the complete arrival and departure duration for one stay in the tour part.

Example:
episodes t s t s t s t s t
durations 10 12 16 90 20 16 10 50 20

the complete arrival duration for the second stay is 10 + 16 = 26
and the complete departure duration is 20 + 10 + 20 = 50
